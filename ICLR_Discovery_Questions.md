# ICELLARÉ Website - Discovery Questions

Hi [Name],

Looking forward to our call! I've put together some questions to help us create the best possible website for ICELLARÉ. Feel free to review these beforehand - we can discuss them during our conversation.

---

## Brand & Positioning

1. What makes ICELLARÉ different from other stem cell/regenerative medicine centers?
2. Who is your ideal client? (Age range, income level, primary health concerns, tech-savviness)
3. What's the primary emotion you want visitors to feel when they land on the site? (Trust, innovation, luxury, hope, etc.)
4. Are there any competitor websites you admire, or ones you'd like to differentiate from?

---

## Content & Messaging

5. Of the 6 services (Stem Cell Banking, Stem Cell Technology, Genetic Testing, Vitamin IV Therapy, Aesthetics, Wellness & Spa), which are most important or profitable to highlight?
6. Do you have patient success stories or testimonials we can feature?
7. What are the top 3 questions prospects ask before booking a consultation?
8. Are there scientific credentials, certifications, awards, or partnerships we should showcase?

---

## Functionality & User Journey

9. What's the primary goal for website visitors? (Book consultation, learn more, contact directly, etc.)
10. Do you need online booking integration? (We can integrate with booking systems)
11. Would you like a chat or voice widget for instant visitor engagement?
12. What forms do you need? (Contact form, consultation request, patient intake, newsletter signup)
13. Is email/newsletter capture important for your marketing?

---

## Visual Assets

14. Do you have professional photos of your facility, staff, and equipment we can use?
15. Do you have any video content? (Lab tours, doctor interviews, procedure explanations)
16. Do you have brand guidelines beyond what's on your current site? (Specific fonts, color codes, tone of voice)
17. Do you have different logo variations available? (Horizontal, stacked, icon-only, etc.)

---

## Technical Requirements

18. What's your ideal timeline and launch deadline?
19. Will you manage content updates internally, or would you like ongoing support?
20. Are there any third-party systems we need to integrate? (CRM, booking platform, patient portal, etc.)
21. Do you have specific analytics or tracking requirements?
22. Do you need the site in multiple languages?

---

## Current Site Feedback

23. What do you like or dislike about your current website?
24. Which pages or services get the most traffic currently?
25. Are there specific SEO keywords or phrases you want to rank for?

---

## Decision Making & Budget

26. Who needs to approve designs and content? (Single decision-maker or committee?)
27. Is there budget flexibility for premium features or integrations?
28. Are you interested in an ongoing maintenance and support contract after launch?
